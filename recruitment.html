Physical Recruitment

At the time of Recruitment SIS Security Ensures that the guards are physically fit. Basic criteria is as follows

  • Minimum height : 162 c.m.
  • Minimum weight : 80 kg.
  • Chest : 79 c.m.(without expansion) and 84 c.m. (on expansion)

Sight

  • If wearing glasses, the glass should not have excess number.

Qualification

  • Minimum Educational Qualification SSC Pass.
